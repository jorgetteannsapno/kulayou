What is seasonal
color analysis?

Developed in the 1980s by Carole Jackson and rooted in Johannes Itten's color theory, seasonal color analysis determines which colors complement your natural coloring — your skin undertone, eye color, and hair color. When you wear your season's colors, your complexion looks clearer, your eyes brighter, and you appear more vibrant.

🌸 Spring

Warm, light, and clear. Golden skin tones, warm eyes. Your colors are peachy, coral, golden yellow — the hues of a warm morning.

🌊 Summer

Cool, soft, and muted. Pink-beige skin, soft eyes. Your palette is rose, lavender, dusty blue — the soft colors of an overcast sky.

🍂 Autumn

Warm, deep, and muted. Golden or olive skin, earthy eyes. Your colors are terra cotta, mustard, forest green — rich harvest tones.

❄️ Winter

Cool, deep, and clear. High contrast features, cool undertones. Your palette is true red, royal blue, pure white — bold and striking.

About Us

Kulay mo.
Color for everyone.

Kulayou — kulay, the Filipino word for color, and you. Your color. Built as a free, non-profit tool by a developer who believes that knowing your colors — and the confidence that comes with it — shouldn't be locked behind a paywall.

Our story

Professional color analysis typically costs thousands of pesos for a single session. The methodology behind it, however, is well-documented, widely studied, and entirely teachable. So we asked: why can't this be free?

Kulayou — from kulay, the Filipino word for color — was built by a not-for-profit developer with a deep passion for making services like this accessible to everyone, regardless of budget, location, or background. We use AI to deliver a full, professional-grade analysis the moment you upload a photo. No waiting. No booking. No bill.

We keep running because of the generosity of people who found this useful and chose to give back — even just a little. Every donation covers server costs and keeps this free for the next person who needs it.

What we believe

Knowing your colors isn't vanity — it's confidence. It's spending less on clothes that don't work and more on things that genuinely suit you. It's walking into a room feeling like yourself. We think everyone deserves that, and we built this so they can have it.

The methodology

Our analysis is grounded in the 12-season system pioneered by Carole Jackson's Color Me Beautiful and expanded by subsequent color theorists. We assess skin undertone, eye clarity, and hair depth to assign a primary and sub-season — the same framework used by professional color consultants worldwide.

Results are AI-generated and intended as a personal guide, not a professional consultation.

Privacy

Your photo.
Your business.

Privacy is a core principle here, not an afterthought. Here is exactly what happens to your data — in plain language.

📸 Your photo is never stored
When you upload a photo, it goes directly to the AI for analysis and is immediately discarded. We have no database of photos. No one at Kulayou ever sees your image. Once your results appear, the photo is gone.
🔒 We collect no personal information
We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or any account details. There is no login. There is no profile. You are anonymous to us by design.
🚫 No tracking or advertising cookies
We use no advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or behavioral analytics. We do not follow you across the web. The only data we see is basic, anonymous site traffic — the kind every website receives — to understand how many people visit. Nothing more.
🤝 We do not sell or share your data
We have nothing to sell. We do not share any information with third parties for advertising or commercial purposes. The only third party involved is Anthropic (the AI provider), which processes your photo solely to generate your analysis and is governed by its own strict privacy policy.
💛 Donations are external and optional
If you choose to donate via GCash, that transaction happens directly between you and our GCash account. We do not process or store any payment information on this site.
✉️ Questions?
If you have any privacy concerns, reach us at hello@kulayou.com. We will always respond.
